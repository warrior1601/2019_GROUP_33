Detailed Description

A point in 3D space with private members of three floating point numbers - X_Coord, Y_Coord, and Z_Coord.

The class has numerous operator overloads, including a vector/cross product overload as the operator*; ability to calculate the scalar product of two vectors; ability to calculate the magitude of a vectors; and ability to get the distance between two vectors.
